Sara'h is a singer of Lebanese descent as she has declared during an interview on Goom Radio.[1] She grew up in the French city of Avignon with her Lebanese father and a French mother and has a brother. Her career jump started after making a cover of "Celui" by Colonel Reyel. Her video got more than 6 million views on YouTube.[2] She has released her first single "Pour Toi" in April 2011.[3] Her follow-up single is "À la faveur de l'automne", a cover of a song by Tété.
